About

The Motif Museum invites visitors to explore the powerful themes within All the Light We Cannot See, Anthony Doerr's novel set during World War II. Through exhibits focused on the journeys of Marie-Laure and Werner, the museum offers a rich, interactive experience centered on themes of survival, resilience, and human connection in the midst of conflict. The museum brings to life the interplay of light and darkness, offering insights into how these elements shaped the characters' lives and their relationships during the war.
